The Art of Brainstorming

Ideation is a crucial phase in the Design Thinking process, where we aim to generate a wide range of creative ideas without immediate judgment or constraints. One of the most effective techniques for ideation is brainstorming.

Brainstorming encourages a free flow of ideas, fostering a collaborative and open-minded environment. During this phase, quantity often leads to quality, and seemingly wild or impractical ideas can spark innovative solutions.

Remember, the goal is to think outside the box and explore a broad spectrum of potential solutions to the defined problem.
